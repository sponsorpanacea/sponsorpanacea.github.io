Past Events

Social Drive

Panacea organised a social drive at Rajkiya Uccha Madhyamik Vidyalaya, at Sanjhriya, Bagru. Many club members volunteered for the drive, and taught via demonstrations, explanations, games, and lots of fun-filled activities. The senior students were also provided with a career counseling session for opportunities and avenues for higher education.

Clone Wars

Clone Wars was organized in the month of October 2022 to promote open source as a concept in the tech community. For beginners, we organized a workshop to introduce them to basics of open source and GitHub. This was followed by monthlong contributions to the Panacea repository.

Panache

Panache was Panacea's flagship event conducted during the Oneiros college fest 2022. The event was conducted in two rounds - an elimination quiz and a jeopardy-themed game show. Participants enthusiastically formed teams of 2 and competed head-to-head for cash prizes.

Sandbox

Panacea hosted Sandbox in November, 2022 to promote game development among tech enthusiasts. Participants were asked to code and create a game based on a central theme and clues.

F.Y.I.

FYI was a round table talk conducted by Panacea in December, 2022. The event invited esteemed panelists -to provide insights on the expectations, working environment and trajectory for getting in the tech industry.

Upcoming Events

WikiRace

Participants surf the Wikipedia ocean as they try to find the quickest route from source to destination. The fastest to navigate through hyperlinks against the ticking clock is crowned the winner.

FIFA Auction

Participants create their ideal squads in FIFA Auction Simulation with virtual currency and compete in FIFA eSport competitions. Variety of tactics and strategies are available for everyone to take part and become champion.

Bench Guess

Participants compete in a challenge that tests their knowledge of tech component benchmarks. It's a format adapted from popular TV shows such as Das Ka Dum, Higher/Lower, and The Price is Right.

Tech Trivia

The name says it all: teams compete in rounds of a trivia based on known and loved popular game shows like Kaun Banega Crorepati, Jeopardy, and Family Feud.

Code Reversal

Participants race against the clock in an effort to reverse-engineer code from the given outputs and resources, spiced up with a pinch of pop culture and memes.
